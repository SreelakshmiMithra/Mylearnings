Recipe 1: Chocolate Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Mix flour, cocoa powder, and sugar
  3. Add eggs and milk, then mix well
  4. Pour the batter into a greased tray
  5. Bake for 30 minutes

Recipe 2: Vanilla Cupcakes

  1. Preheat the oven to 170°C
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl
  3. Add butter and vanilla essence
  4. Fill cupcake liners with batter
  5. Bake for 20 minutes

Recipe 3: Bread Toast

  1. Switch on the toaster
  2. Place bread slices inside
  3. Toast until golden brown
  4. Apply butter evenly
  5. Serve hot

Recipe 4: Lemon Tea

  1. Boil water in a pan
  2. Add tea powder
  3. Strain the tea into a cup
  4. Add lemon juice
  5. Serve warm

Recipe 5: Fruit Salad (Default)

  1. Wash all fruits properly
  2. Cut fruits into small pieces
  3. Add honey and lemon juice
  4. Mix gently
  5. Serve fresh
